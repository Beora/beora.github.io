We work as a team, and always will be

Doing Iterative Development adds agility to the development process. We divide our development schedule into about iterations of 1 week in length. One week is the best choice even though it seems very short.

Helping Choosing the right backlog items according to your product vision to build the right product.

Having tight feedback loops to ensure that your software is always in sync with evolving needs.

communicating face to face daily. We will work together on everything from requirements to code.

partaking in a retrospective meeting at the end of each iteration. The team will reflect on how everything went and then decides what changes they want to make in the next iteration.

striving to build, test, and release software faster and more frequently. This approach helps reduce the cost, time, and risk of delivering changes by allowing for more incremental updates to applications in production.
